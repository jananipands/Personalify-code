personalify

Information Usage

personalify is a web application that determines the user's personality traits based on their top listened tracks in Spotify. The user will need a Spotify account (either free or premium) to log into personalify. Using Spotify's Web API, personalify gathers the user's top tracks as well as each track's audio features from their Spotify account. Additionally, personalify will gather the user's full name, Spotify username, and profile picture to be featured the user's private dashboard.

Trait Determination

personalify focuses on four audio features: Danceability, Valence, Energy, and Instrumentalness. According to new studies on music and personality, the four audio features correlate strongly to extraversion, outlook, energy, and taste/preference. For each of the user's top tracks, personalify calculates the mean scores for each audio feature and uses the below scales to determine the corresponding personality trait.
